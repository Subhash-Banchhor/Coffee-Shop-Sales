# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard Analysis

An interactive Excel dashboard project focused on analyzing coffee shop sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends using data visualization and KPI analysis.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was created to transform raw coffee shop transaction data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard. The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer footfall, product demand, and operational trends across different store locations.

The dashboard allows users to explore sales patterns dynamically using filters and visual reports, helping identify opportunities for business improvement and better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and customer activity
* Identify peak business hours and customer ordering trends
* Compare store-wise sales and footfall performance
* Understand customer size preferences and product demand
* Highlight top-performing products and categories
* Create a visually interactive business dashboard using Excel

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Peak Customer Activity During Morning Hours

Orders increased significantly between 7 AM and 10 AM, indicating strong morning demand and commuter purchasing behavior.

### Coffee Products Generated Highest Revenue

Coffee contributed the largest share of category sales, making it the primary revenue-driving product segment.

### Hell’s Kitchen Recorded Highest Sales

Among all store locations, Hell’s Kitchen generated the highest revenue and customer footfall.

### Regular and Large Sizes Were Most Preferred

Most customers preferred Regular and Large beverage sizes, accounting for the majority of total orders.

### Friday Had Highest Customer Footfall

Customer visits were highest on Fridays, showing stronger weekday business activity compared to weekends.

### Barista Espresso Was the Top-Selling Product

Barista Espresso generated the highest sales among all products, contributing significantly to overall revenue.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase staffing during morning rush hours to improve operational efficiency.
* Focus promotional campaigns on high-performing products such as Barista Espresso and Brewed Chai Tea.
* Introduce weekend offers to improve Saturday customer footfall.
* Prioritize inventory management for coffee and tea categories due to higher demand.
* Apply successful sales strategies from Hell’s Kitchen across other store locations.
